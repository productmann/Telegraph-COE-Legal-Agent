Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy: 
a) Client Cancellation: 
• Cancellation by Chelsea Magazines up to four weeks prior to any scheduled session - no charge • Cancellation by Chelsea Magazines within four weeks prior to any scheduled session - 100% of applicable session fee • Chelsea Magazines may reschedule sessions with minimum four weeks’ notice at no additional charge, subject to MST  trainer availability 
b) Trainer Cancellation: 
• If MST cancels any scheduled session due to trainer illness, emergency, or other unavoidable circumstances, the  session will be rescheduled at no additional cost to Chelsea Magazines 
• MST will aim to provide minimum 24 hours’ notice of cancellation where-ever possible 
• If rescheduling is not possible within a reasonable timeframe, Chelsea Magazines will receive a full refund for that  specific session 
• MST will make every reasonable effort to provide a suitable replacement trainer of equivalent experience and  expertise if required 
6. MATERIALS 
Delegates will receive supporting summary slides from each workshop and will complete regular personal action plans 
7. CONFIDENTIALITY 
Both parties agree to keep confidential any business information shared during the training programme. This includes  client data, sales strategies, and proprietary training materials. Neither party will disclose such information to third  parties without written consent. Chelsea Magazines agrees that all MST training materials are for the exclusive use of  the attending delegates and may not be shared, distributed, copied, or re-used for training other personnel without  prior written permission from MST. 
8. AGREEMENT 
This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Any modifications must be agreed in writing by  both parties.
